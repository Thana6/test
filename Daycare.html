WHAT TO BRING

First-Day Handover Checklist

Three short lists so the first drop-off feels easy, not overwhelming.

Documents

  • ✅ Completed application form
  • ✅ Child's birth certificate copy
  • ✅ Parent / guardian ID copy
  • ✅ Emergency contact list
  • ✅ Medical records & immunisations
  • ✅ Allergy or special-needs info

Essential Supplies

  • ✅ Extra clothes (5–7 sets)
  • ✅ Diapers / wipes, if needed
  • ✅ Bottles & formula milk
  • ✅ Sippy cup or water bottle
  • ✅ A comfort item — blanket or toy
  • ✅ Labelled food containers

Medication & Communication

  • ✅ Doctor-prescribed medication with instructions
  • ✅ A notebook or communication folder
  • ✅ Drop-off / pickup authorisation list
