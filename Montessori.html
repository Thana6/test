LKG — Lower Kindergarten

Age group: 3.5 – 4.5 years

Stationery & materials to bring:

  • Drawing book (large) – 1, crayons – 1 set, colour pencils (12) – 1 set
  • Bristol board – 2, pencils – 6, erasers – 3, sharpener
  • Scissors, small ruler, binder gum (big), scrap book
  • Double-rule book (Tamil) – 1, ½-inch square book (Maths) – 1, red-rule book (English) – 1
  • Art supplies: tissue paper (5 colours), varnish paper (5 colours), A4 sheets (15 white + 5 of each colour), clay set, fabric paint, paint brushes
  • Glass bag, pencil box
UKG — Upper Kindergarten

Age group: 4.5 – 5.5 years

Stationery & materials to bring:

  • Bristol board – 3, pencils – 1 box, colour pencil box (12), sharpener
  • Binder gum (big) – 1, scissors, erasers – 3
  • Varnish paper (6 colours), tissue paper (6 colours)
  • A4 white – 12, A4 colour – 6 colours (2 sets each)
  • Scrap book – 1, double-rule books – 2 (100 pages each)
  • ½-inch square book – 1 (100 pages), drawing book – 1 (80 pages)
  • A picture book covering trees, family, the human body, fruits, vegetables, birds, animals, homes, countries, and community helpers
  • Glass bag, pencil box
